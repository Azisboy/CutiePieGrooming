About Us

Our Story

With over 15 years of professional experience in Los Angeles's vibrant pet grooming industry, we brought our expertise to Clayton in 2023. Our journey is built on a foundation of extensive experience, dedication to pet care, and countless happy furry clients from Beverly Hills to the San Fernando Valley.

Our Mission

Drawing from our extensive Los Angeles experience, we're committed to bringing premium, West Coast-style pet grooming services to Clayton. We understand that each pet is unique, and we use our years of expertise to provide personalized care that ensures both comfort and style.

Frequently Asked Questions

Vaccination Requirements

For the safety of all our furry friends, we maintain strict vaccination policies:

  • Rabies vaccination is mandatory for all pets aged 5 months and older
  • Puppies must have completed their second round of vaccinations with a 10-day waiting period
  • We highly encourage complete vaccination coverage for optimal pet health and safety

No Show and Late Policy

To maintain our high-quality service standards:

  • Please provide at least 2 hours notice for appointment cancellations
  • If you're running more than 15 minutes late, contact your groomer immediately
  • Late arrivals may require rescheduling to ensure quality service for all clients

Drop-off/Pick-up Times

We're committed to efficient and flexible scheduling:

  • Standard grooming appointments typically take 1-3 hours
  • Express grooming services available upon request at booking
  • Extended stay accommodations can be arranged during check-in if needed
  • Our priority is ensuring quality care while minimizing your pet's time away from home
